Camping 2022

Campsite location

The Troops First Campout will be at Lost Ridge Group Camp in Oakland, CA. It is located near the center of Anthony Chabot Regional Park. Further Detailed information will be provided as we get closer to the event.

October 15th and 16th, 2022.

*********the last day to sign up for this event will be October 7th**************

Cubs, Families, Siblings, Other Family Members and Friends are invited.

The Campout starts at 3PM -but come up anytime your family needs and leave when you want. Come up after a Soccer Game and Leave before Church, or stay the entire time, the choice is up to you.

There is no charge for this event. This campout is paid for by your annual dues.The pack will provide Dinner and Desert Saturday Night followed by Breakfast Sunday Morning. All other snacks and dietary required foods are up to the families.

Please sign up with the total number of people in your party AND a volunteer slot [One Volunteer Slot Per Family please.] In other words there are two things to sign up for: the number of people and a volunteer slot. Non volunteers will be assigned a duty.

This is not a screen friendly event. In the event that all slots fill up - send me a message.

Please add attendees & volunteer names in the comments section.

Created by:   Gary Langston
