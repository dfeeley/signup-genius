Warrriors Basketball Game

Come Join Pack 645 to Watch the Golden State Warriors  take on the Minnesota Timberwolves on March 26th, 2023.

Cubs, Parents, Siblings - all Welcome.   We have a limit of 100 Tickets.

The Warriors will play in the Chase Center in San Francisco, CA on Sun, Mar 26, 2023 - 5:30PM.

Just sign up with the quantity in your party, add your email address to the My Comment section, and Pay!  Tickets will be handed out, later over the Warriors App - More information to come.

Please pay for all members in your party and add your email address in the comment slot.
